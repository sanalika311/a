<h1>INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY</h1>

<p>
  Information technology helps us communicate, work and learn faster.
  It connects people around the world and makes life easier.
  Computers and the Internet are part of our everyday life.
  New technologies appear every year.
  IT continues to grow and change the world.
</p>

<div class="row">
  <div class="column">
    <h2>Artificial Intelligence</h2>
    <p>AI helps machines learn and make smart decisions. It supports healthcare with faster diagnosis and improves education with personalized learning. Many apps use AI every day, often without us noticing.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="column">
    <h2>Cybersecurity</h2>
    <p>Cybersecurity keeps our data and privacy safe online. Strong passwords and updates protect devices from attacks. Companies use security tools to detect and stop threats quickly.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="column">
    <h2>Cloud Computing</h2>
    <p>The cloud lets us access files anywhere, anytime. Teams can share documents and work together in real time. It also saves costs by removing the need for expensive hardware.</p>
  </div>
</div>
